About us

Our Mission

You're Not Alone

We want parents to feel a sense of community and companionship throughout their journey. Parenthood can be both thrilling and daunting, and no one should feel isolated on this incredible ride.

Access Made Easy

We are committed to making the journey smoother for parents by providing easy access to the information, resources, and support they need during pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond.

Reducing Mortality

We believe that by empowering parents with knowledge, support, and accessible resources, we can positively impact the health outcomes of both mothers and infants.
